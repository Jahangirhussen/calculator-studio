Convert between pascal, bar, psi and standard atmosphere.

Formula

value_in_base = value × factor(unit → pascal)
result = value_in_base ÷ factor(target → pascal)

Example

Convert 1 atm to psi: 1 atm × 101325 = 101325 Pa. 101325 ÷ 6894.757293 = 14.695949 psi.
