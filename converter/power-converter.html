Power Converter

Convert between watts, kilowatts, horsepower and megawatts.

Result
—

Formula

value_in_base = value × factor(unit → watt)
result = value_in_base ÷ factor(target → watt)

Example

Convert 10 horsepower to kilowatts: 10 hp × 745.699872 = 7456.99872 W. 7456.99872 ÷ 1000 = 7.45700 kW.

How To Use

Enter a value, choose the unit you're converting from and the unit you want, then click Convert.
