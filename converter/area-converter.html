Area Converter

Convert between square meters, square kilometers, square feet, square yards, acres and hectares.

Formula

value_in_base = value × factor(unit → sq meter)
result = value_in_base ÷ factor(target → sq meter)

Example

Convert 2 acres to hectares: 2 acre × 4046.8564224 = 8093.712845 m². 8093.712845 m² ÷ 10000 = 0.809371 hectares.

How To Use

Enter a value, choose the unit you're converting from and the unit you want, then click Convert.
