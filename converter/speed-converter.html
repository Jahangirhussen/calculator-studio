Speed Converter

Convert between meters per second, kilometers per hour, miles per hour and knots.

Result
—

Formula

value_in_base = value × factor(unit → m/s)
result = value_in_base ÷ factor(target → m/s)

Example

Convert 100 km/h to mph: 100 × 0.277778 = 27.7778 m/s. 27.7778 ÷ 0.44704 = 62.137119 mph.

How To Use

Enter a value, choose the unit you're converting from and the unit you want, then click Convert.
