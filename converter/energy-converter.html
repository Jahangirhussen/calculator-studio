Energy Converter

Convert between joules, calories, kilocalories and kilowatt-hours.

Result
—

Formula

value_in_base = value × factor(unit → joule)
result = value_in_base ÷ factor(target → joule)

Example

Convert 1 kWh to kilocalories: 1 kWh × 3600000 = 3600000 J. 3600000 ÷ 4184 = 860.421 kcal.

How To Use

Enter a value, choose the unit you're converting from and the unit you want, then click Convert.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the base unit used for energy conversion? ▾
This tool converts every unit to joules first, then to your target unit.
How many joules are in a kilocalorie? ▾
One kilocalorie (food Calorie) equals 4184 joules.
