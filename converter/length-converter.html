Length Converter

Convert between millimeters, centimeters, meters, kilometers, inches, feet, yards and miles.

Result
—

Formula

value_in_base = value × factor(unit → meter)
result = value_in_base ÷ factor(target → meter)

Example

Convert 5 kilometers to miles: 5 km × 1000 = 5000 m. 5000 m ÷ 1609.344 = 3.106856 miles.

How To Use

Enter a value, choose the unit you're converting from and the unit you want, then click Convert.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the base unit used for length conversion? ▾
This tool converts every unit to meters first, then from meters to your target unit, which keeps the math accurate for any combination.
How many meters are in a mile? ▾
One mile equals exactly 1609.344 meters.
