About Calculator Studio

Calculator Studio is a free, all-in-one calculator platform covering math, finance, health, conversions, education, engineering, programming, time and everyday utilities. Every calculator runs entirely in your browser — no server, no tracking, no data collection.

Our Mission

To provide fast, accurate, and accessible calculation tools for everyone — students, professionals and everyday users — completely free of charge.

Why Calculator Studio?

150+ calculators, clean design, dark and light themes, instant results, and full mobile responsiveness. Built with plain HTML, CSS and JavaScript for maximum speed and reliability.
