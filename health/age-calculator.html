Age Calculator

Calculate your exact age in years, months and days from your birthdate to any target date.

Result
—

Formula

Age = Target Date − Date of Birth (calculated as full years, then remaining months, then remaining days)

Example

Born on 15 June 2000, on 9 July 2026 you would be 26 years, 0 months, 24 days old.

How To Use

Pick your date of birth, leave "Age at Date" as today or choose another date, then click Calculate.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is age calculated in years, months and days? ▾
We count the number of complete years between the two dates, then the remaining complete months, then the remaining days — accounting for varying month lengths and leap years.
Can I calculate my age on a future or past date? ▾
Yes, change the "Age at Date" field to any date to see your age at that point in time.
