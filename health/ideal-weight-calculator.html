Formula

Devine Formula (height over 5 ft, per inch): Male: Ideal Weight = 50 kg + 2.3 kg × inches over 5 ft Female: Ideal Weight = 45.5 kg + 2.3 kg × inches over 5 ft (For height at or under 5 ft, no addition is applied)

Example

A male 180 cm tall (≈70.9 in, 10.9 in over 5 ft): Ideal Weight = 50 + 2.3×10.9 ≈ 75.1 kg.

How To Use

Select gender, enter height in centimeters, then click Calculate to see your estimated ideal weight.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Devine formula accurate for everyone? ▾
The Devine formula is a general estimate originally developed for medication dosing and doesn't account for muscle mass, frame size or body composition.
What if I'm shorter than 5 feet? ▾
For heights at or below 5 feet (152.4 cm), the calculator uses the base weight (50 kg for men, 45.5 kg for women) without any addition.
