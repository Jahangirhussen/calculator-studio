Formula

Metric: BMI = kg / (m)² Imperial: BMI = 703 × lb / (in)² Underweight: < 18.5 Normal: 18.5 – 24.9 Overweight: 25 – 29.9 Obese: 30+

Example

A person weighing 70 kg at 175 cm has a BMI of 70 / (1.75)² = 22.9, which is in the Normal range.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is BMI accurate for everyone? ▾
BMI is a general screening tool and does not directly measure body fat. It may be less accurate for athletes, the elderly, or pregnant women.
What is a healthy BMI range? ▾
A BMI between 18.5 and 24.9 is generally considered a healthy weight range for most adults.
