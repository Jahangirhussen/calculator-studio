Formula

Base Water Intake (L/day) = Weight (kg) × 0.033 Adjusted Intake = Base Intake × Activity Multiplier Sedentary ×1.0, Light ×1.2, Moderate ×1.4, Active ×1.6, Very Active ×1.8

Example

A 70 kg person with moderate activity: 70 × 0.033 = 2.31 L base, × 1.4 = 3.23 L/day recommended.

How To Use

Enter your weight in kilograms, select your activity level, then click Calculate to see your daily water intake recommendation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does climate affect water needs? ▾
Yes, hot or humid climates increase fluid loss through sweat, so you may need more water than this baseline estimate suggests.
Does all fluid intake need to be plain water? ▾
No, water from foods and other beverages also contributes to your total daily fluid intake, though plain water is generally the best primary source.
