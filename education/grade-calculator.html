Formula

Percentage = (Marks Obtained / Total Marks) × 100 A+ : 90 - 100 A : 80 - 89 B : 70 - 79 C : 60 - 69 D : 50 - 59 F : below 50

Example

If you scored 82 out of 100, your percentage is 82% which falls in the A grade range.

How To Use

Enter the marks obtained and the total marks, then click Calculate to see your percentage and letter grade.

Frequently Asked Questions

What percentage is required to pass? ▾
This calculator uses 50% as the passing threshold (grade D). Below 50% is an F.
Can I use a different grading scale? ▾
This calculator uses a standard A+ to F scale. Your institution may use a different scale.
