Attendance Calculator

Calculate your current attendance percentage and find out how many more classes to attend, or how many you can miss, to hit your target attendance.

Result
—

Formula

Current % = Attended / Total × 100 If below target, classes needed (x) to attend consecutively: x = (Target×Total − 100×Attended) / (100 − Target) If above target, classes you can still miss (y): y = (100×Attended / Target) − Total

Example

If you attended 60 of 80 classes (75%) and your target is 80%, you need to attend 20 more consecutive classes to reach 80%.

How To Use

Enter classes attended, total classes held, and your required attendance percentage, then click Calculate.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if my attendance is already above target? ▾
The calculator will tell you how many classes you can afford to miss going forward while staying at or above your target.
Does this assume future classes are attended consecutively? ▾
Yes, the "classes needed" figure assumes you attend every future class until you reach the target.
