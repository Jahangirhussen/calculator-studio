Marks Percentage Calculator

Calculate your percentage score from marks obtained and total marks.

Result
—

Formula

Percentage = (Marks Obtained / Total Marks) × 100

Example

If you scored 450 out of 500 marks, your percentage is (450 / 500) × 100 = 90%.

How To Use

Enter the marks obtained and the total marks, then click Calculate to see your percentage.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is marks percentage calculated? ▾
Divide the marks obtained by the total marks and multiply by 100 to get the percentage.
Can total marks be zero? ▾
No, total marks must be greater than zero to calculate a valid percentage.
