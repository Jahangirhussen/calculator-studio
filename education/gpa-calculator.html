GPA Calculator

Calculate your Grade Point Average by adding each course's credit hours and grade point (0-4 scale).

Result
—

Formula

GPA = Σ(Credit Hours × Grade Point) / Σ(Credit Hours)

Example

Course A: 3 credits, 4.0 GP. Course B: 4 credits, 3.0 GP. GPA = (3×4 + 4×3) / (3+4) = 24/7 = 3.43

How To Use

Enter each course's credit hours and grade point. Click "Add Course" to add more rows, then click Calculate.

Frequently Asked Questions

What grade point scale is used? ▾
This calculator uses the standard 0-4.0 grade point scale common at most universities.
How many courses can I add? ▾
You can add unlimited course rows using the "Add Course" button.
