CGPA Calculator

Calculate your Cumulative GPA across semesters using each semester's GPA and credit hours.

Result
—

Formula

CGPA = Σ(Semester GPA × Credit Hours) / Σ(Credit Hours)

Example

Semester 1: GPA 3.5, 15 credits. Semester 2: GPA 3.8, 16 credits. CGPA = (3.5×15 + 3.8×16) / (15+16) = 113.3/31 = 3.65

How To Use

Enter each semester's GPA and credit hours. Click "Add Semester" to add more rows, then click Calculate.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between GPA and CGPA? ▾
GPA measures performance in a single semester, while CGPA is the weighted average across all semesters.
How many semesters can I add? ▾
You can add unlimited semester rows using the "Add Semester" button.
