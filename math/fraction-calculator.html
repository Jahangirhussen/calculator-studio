Add, subtract, multiply and divide fractions, simplified automatically.

Formula

a/b + c/d = (ad + cb) / bd a/b × c/d = ac / bd a/b ÷ c/d = ad / bc Simplified by dividing by the GCD.

Example

1/2 + 1/3 = 5/6. The result is automatically reduced to lowest terms.

How To Use

Enter numerator and denominator for each fraction, choose an operation, then click Calculate.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does it simplify the result? ▾
Yes, the fraction is automatically reduced to its lowest terms using the greatest common divisor.
What if a denominator is zero? ▾
A denominator of zero is invalid and the calculator will show an error.
