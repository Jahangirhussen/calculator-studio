Power Calculator

Compute base raised to an exponent (b^e), including negative and fractional exponents.

Result
—

Formula

Result = base^exponent

Example

2^10 = 1024. 27^(1/3) = 3.

How To Use

Enter the base and exponent, then click Calculate.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can the exponent be negative? ▾
Yes, a negative exponent gives the reciprocal, e.g. 2^-2 = 0.25.
Can the exponent be a fraction? ▾
Yes, fractional exponents compute roots, e.g. 8^(1/3) = 2.
