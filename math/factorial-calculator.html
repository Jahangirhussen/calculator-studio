Factorial Calculator

Compute n! (factorial) for any non-negative integer, including very large numbers.

Result
—

Formula

n! = n × (n−1) × (n−2) × ... × 1 0! = 1

Example

5! = 5 × 4 × 3 × 2 × 1 = 120.

How To Use

Enter a non-negative integer (up to 5000) and click Calculate. Uses BigInt for exact large results.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is 0!? ▾
0! is defined to be 1.
Why is there a max limit of 5000? ▾
Factorials grow extremely fast; 5000! already has thousands of digits, which is a practical browser computation limit.
