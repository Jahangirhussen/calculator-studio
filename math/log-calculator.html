Formula

How To Use

Enter x (must be greater than 0) and the base, then click Calculate. Use base ≈2.71828 for natural log.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if x is 0 or negative? ▾
Logarithms are only defined for positive x; the calculator will show an error.
How do I compute natural log (ln)? ▾
Set the base to Euler's number e ≈ 2.718281828.
