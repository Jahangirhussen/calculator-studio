Ratio Calculator

Simplify a ratio A:B, or solve for a missing value in A:B = C:D.

Result
—

Formula

Simplify: divide A and B by GCD(A,B) Proportion: A/B = C/D → solve the missing term by cross multiplication

Example

Simplify 8:12 → 2:3. Solve 2:3 = 10:? → ? = 15.

How To Use

Choose a mode. For proportion mode, leave exactly one of A, B, C, D blank to solve for it.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is a ratio simplified? ▾
Both terms are divided by their greatest common divisor.
Can I solve for any term in a proportion? ▾
Yes, leave the unknown term blank and the calculator solves it using cross multiplication.
