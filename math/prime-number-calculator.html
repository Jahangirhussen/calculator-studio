Prime Number Calculator

Check whether a number is prime, and see its factors.

Result
—

Formula

A number n > 1 is prime if it has no divisors other than 1 and itself. Checked by trial division up to √n.

Example

17 is prime. 18 is not prime (factors: 1, 2, 3, 6, 9, 18).

How To Use

Enter a non-negative integer and click Calculate.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is 1 a prime number? ▾
No, 1 is neither prime nor composite by definition.
Is 2 the only even prime? ▾
Yes, 2 is the only even prime number; all other even numbers are divisible by 2.
