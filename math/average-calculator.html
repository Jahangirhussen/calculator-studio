Average Calculator

Calculate mean, median and mode of a list of numbers.

Result
—

Formula

Mean = Sum of values / Count Median = middle value (sorted) Mode = most frequently occurring value

Example

For 4, 8, 15, 16, 23, 42: Mean ≈ 18, Median = 15.5, Mode = none (all unique).

How To Use

Enter a list of numbers separated by commas or spaces, then click Calculate.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if there is no mode? ▾
If all values occur equally often, the calculator reports "No unique mode".
How is the median calculated for an even count? ▾
It is the average of the two middle values after sorting.
