LCM Calculator

Find the least common multiple of two or more positive integers.

Result
—

Formula

LCM(a,b) = (a × b) / GCD(a,b) For more numbers, LCM is applied pairwise across the list.

Example

LCM(4, 6, 8) = 24.

How To Use

Enter two or more positive integers separated by commas, then click Calculate.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I enter more than two numbers? ▾
Yes, enter any number of comma-separated positive integers.
What is LCM used for? ▾
LCM is commonly used to find common denominators when adding or comparing fractions.
