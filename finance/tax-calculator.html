Tax Calculator

Calculate the tax amount owed and your net income after tax based on a flat percentage rate.

Result
—

Formula

Tax = Income × Rate / 100
Net Income = Income − Tax

Example

Gross income $60,000 at 20% tax rate: Tax = 60000 × 20/100 = $12,000. Net income = $48,000.

How To Use

Enter your gross income and applicable flat tax rate percentage, then click Calculate to see the tax owed and your net take-home income.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does this account for tax brackets/slabs? ▾
No, this calculator applies a single flat tax rate for simplicity. Actual tax systems often use progressive brackets with different rates for different income ranges.
Is this calculator suitable for official tax filing? ▾
No, it's meant for quick estimates only. Consult a tax professional or official tax authority calculator for accurate filing figures.
