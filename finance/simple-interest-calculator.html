Simple Interest Calculator

Calculate simple interest and total amount on a principal over a given time and rate.

Result
—

Formula

SI = P × R × T / 100
Total Amount = P + SI

Example

Principal $10,000 at 5% for 3 years: SI = 10000 × 5 × 3 / 100 = $1,500. Total amount = $11,500.

How To Use

Enter the principal amount, annual interest rate, and time period in years, then click Calculate to see the simple interest and total amount.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is simple interest? ▾
Simple interest is calculated only on the original principal amount, unlike compound interest which is calculated on principal plus accumulated interest.
Where is simple interest commonly used? ▾
Simple interest is often used for short-term loans, car loans, and some savings certificates.
