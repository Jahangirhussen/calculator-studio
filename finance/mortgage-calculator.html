Mortgage Calculator

Estimate your monthly mortgage payment after accounting for your down payment.

Formula

Principal = Home Price − Down Payment
Payment = P × r × (1+r)n / ((1+r)n − 1)
r = monthly rate = annual rate / 12 / 100, n = months

Example

Home price $350,000 with $70,000 down payment leaves a principal of $280,000. At 6.5% over 30 years, monthly payment ≈ $1,769.61.

How To Use

Enter the home price, down payment, annual interest rate, and loan term, then click Calculate to view your monthly mortgage payment, total interest, and total repayment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does this include taxes and insurance? ▾
No, this calculator estimates principal and interest only. Property tax, insurance, and HOA fees are not included.
How does a bigger down payment help? ▾
A larger down payment reduces the loan principal, lowering both your monthly payment and total interest paid over the loan term.
