ROI Calculator

Calculate your Return on Investment (ROI) percentage and net gain.

Result
—

Formula

ROI (%) = (Gain − Cost) / Cost × 100
Net Profit = Gain − Cost

Example

Investment cost $1,000, final value $1,300: Net profit = $300. ROI = (1300−1000)/1000 × 100 = 30%.

How To Use

Enter your original investment cost and the final value (or total returns), then click Calculate to see your ROI percentage and net profit.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a good ROI? ▾
A "good" ROI depends on the investment type and time period, but a positive ROI above inflation and market averages is generally considered favorable.
Can ROI be negative? ▾
Yes, if the final value is lower than the investment cost, ROI will be negative, indicating a loss.
