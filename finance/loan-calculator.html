Loan Calculator

Calculate your loan's monthly payment, total interest, and total repayment amount.

Result
—

Formula

Payment = P × r × (1+r)n / ((1+r)n − 1)
r = monthly rate = annual rate / 12 / 100, n = months

Example

A loan of $200,000 at 7.5% for 10 years (120 months): monthly payment ≈ $2,374.53, total payment ≈ $284,943, total interest ≈ $84,943.

How To Use

Enter loan amount, annual interest rate, and term in years, then click Calculate to view your monthly payment, total interest, and total repayment.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is a loan payment calculated? ▾
Loan payments are calculated using the amortization formula, which spreads principal and interest evenly across all payments so each installment is the same amount.
What's the difference between loan and EMI calculators? ▾
They use the same math — EMI is simply the term commonly used in South Asia for a fixed monthly loan installment.
