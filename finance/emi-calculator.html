EMI Calculator

Calculate your monthly loan EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) along with total interest and total payment.

Result
—

Formula

EMI = P × r × (1+r)n / ((1+r)n − 1)
r = monthly rate = annual rate / 12 / 100, n = number of months

Example

For a loan of ₹500,000 at 9% annual interest for 5 years (60 months): r = 0.0075, n = 60. EMI ≈ ₹10,378.85, total payment ≈ ₹622,731, total interest ≈ ₹122,731.

How To Use

Enter the loan amount, annual interest rate, and tenure (in years or months), then click Calculate to see your monthly EMI, total interest paid, and total payment over the loan term.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is EMI? ▾
EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is the fixed amount a borrower pays every month to repay a loan, covering both principal and interest.
Does a longer tenure reduce EMI? ▾
Yes, a longer tenure lowers the monthly EMI but increases the total interest paid over the life of the loan.
