Currency Converter

Convert an amount from one currency to another using an exchange rate you enter manually.

Result
—

Formula

Converted Amount = Amount × Exchange Rate

Example

Converting 100 USD to EUR at a rate of 0.92: 100 × 0.92 = 92 EUR.

How To Use

Since this calculator works offline without a live API, enter the amount, the currency codes for reference, and the current exchange rate (which you can find from your bank or a currency site), then click Convert.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I need to enter the exchange rate manually? ▾
This calculator works fully offline without external APIs, so you provide the current exchange rate from your preferred source to get an accurate conversion.
Where can I find current exchange rates? ▾
Banks, currency exchange services, and financial news sites publish daily exchange rates you can use here.
